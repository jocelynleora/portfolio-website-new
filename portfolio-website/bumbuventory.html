INVENTORY WEBSITE

BUMBUVENTORY

A website that helps the Bumbu 47 company manage its inventory. This website was made for the purpose of a team project for our final exam for the ‘System Analysis and Design’ subject.

DASHBOARD

First thing you will see after login.


You can see some graphs about low stocks, best seller items, production vs sales, etc.

DASHBOARD (light mode)

We also provide the light mode version!


CUSTOMER LIST

We collect the customers' list here, by their Name, Tax ID, and Address.


BATCH LIST

Feature to record new batches every new production


VARIANT LIST

The variants of the product.


It can be created, edited, and deleted

SALES INVOICE

To create the sales invoices.


It can also be printed into PDF!

Role Designer
Project Summary The Process: 1. Planning & Fact Finding - We found a spice company in need of a website/application and researched their competitors, analyzed their issues, and brainstormed solutions after conducting an interview and reviewing their documents. 2. Database, Design, and Prototype - We created an inventory website for a spice company with a MySQL Workbench database and a user flow featuring sections for Dashboard, Customer Management, Inventory Management, Finance Management, and System Management. 3. Testing - Then, we asked the co-owner to do the user testing. And to make it simpler, we made a User Test Log.
